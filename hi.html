About Jordan Supermarket

Your trusted partner for quality shopping

Jordan Supermarket has been serving the community since 1995. We started as a small neighborhood grocery store and have grown into a full-service supermarket with a dedicated games section.

Our mission is to provide high-quality products at affordable prices while delivering exceptional customer service. We source our groceries from trusted local farmers and suppliers to ensure freshness and quality.

Our games section features the latest video games, consoles, and accessories for all major platforms. Our knowledgeable staff can help you find the perfect game for your preferences.
